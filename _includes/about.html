About

I am a Trustee of the charity codebar. codebar started out of recognition that there is a shortage of women, non-binary, LGBTQ+, and people belonging to underrepresented ethnic groups in tech. We are a resource for students who cannot afford formal training through coding bootcamps or university degrees, for financial reasons or otherwise. Over 10 years, we have built a global ‘codebar Community’ of 25,000+ people of all backgrounds can access free training and total tech support.

I have also given many talks on diversity and inclusion, building communities and how to effectively bring in junior developers to your team. I've also run several technical workshops at events. I would love to do more of this! If you're interested, please get in touch.

I also get excited about building great products and working with great companies. I work with small and medium-sized businesses, design teams and agencies, building fast and responsive websites.
